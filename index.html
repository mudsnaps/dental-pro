FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the first consultation really free?
Yes, completely free and with no obligation. We'll assess your dental health and discuss treatment options without any pressure. You decide what you'd like to proceed with.
Are your treatments painful?
We use the latest anaesthetic techniques and gentle methods to ensure your treatment is as comfortable as possible. Most patients are surprised at how painless modern dentistry is. If you feel anxious, just tell us — we'll take extra care.
Do you offer EMI or payment plans?
Yes. We offer 0% EMI on treatments above ₹5,000 through major banks and UPI credit. We also have monthly care plans starting at ₹999/month that cover routine care. Ask us on WhatsApp for details.
How long does a dental implant take?
The implant placement itself takes about 60–90 minutes. Then there's a healing period of 3–6 months while the implant fuses with your jawbone. The final crown is then fitted, completing the process. We guide you through every stage.
What should I do in a dental emergency?
Call us immediately on +91 70000 00000 or WhatsApp us. We keep same-day emergency slots available every day. For severe pain or swelling, call us directly — don't wait for an online booking.
